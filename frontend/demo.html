AI Transparency
Responsible AI evaluation.
Built on six principles that ensure every evaluation is fair, auditable, and human-validated.
1
Evidence-Based Scoring
Every score is backed by direct quotes from the student transcript. No black-box grading.
2
Rubric-Governed
All evaluations grounded in your published rubric. AI matches evidence — never invents it.
3
Confidence Scoring
Every evaluation includes a confidence score. Below 60% is auto-flagged for instructor review.
4
Instructor Override
Every AI score can be reviewed and overridden. AI assists — educators decide. Always.
5
Data Privacy
Student data processed locally. No external storage. Exports download to your machine.
6
Consistency & Fairness
Every student evaluated against the same rubric by the same model. Eliminates evaluator bias.
Confidence Thresholds
80–100%
High Confidence
Clear evidence found. Score is reliable.
60–79%
Medium Confidence
Some ambiguity present. Review recommended.
Below 60%
Low Confidence — Flagged
Instructor review required.
Note: High confidence on a low score means the AI is certain the student underperformed — not that they did well. 0 questions = zero ambiguity = high confidence.
